ABOUT US

Naturals Ice Cream: If you’re looking for a unique and original ice cream experience, Naturals Ice Cream is worth trying. They use only three key ingredients: fresh fruits, milk, and sugar. Their fruit-based flavors, such as Mango, Sitaphal (custard apple), Strawberry, and more, are a testament to their commitment to quality and taste1. Classic Flavors: Some classic ice cream flavors that have stood the test of time include: Mint Chocolate Chip: Refreshing mint combined with chocolate chips. Vanilla: A timeless favorite, simple yet versatile. Chocolate: Rich and indulgent. Cookies N’ Cream: A delightful blend of chocolate cookies and creamy vanilla. Strawberry: Bursting with fruity goodness.
